About us

At Raindays, we are passionate about producing the highest-quality rain jackets for our customers. We pride ourselves on meticulous attention to detail and strive to create products that withstand any weather condition you encounter in your adventures. Our commitment to quality starts with our material selection process. We source premium, waterproof fabrics that are not only highly functional but also comfortable to wear. With features such as taped seams and adjustable hoods, we ensure that our jackets keep you dry even in the heaviest downpours. Because of this we also have a 5 year warranty on all of our jackets.
